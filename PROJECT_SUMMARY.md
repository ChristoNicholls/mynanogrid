- **Model:** Huawei SUN2000-3KTL-L1

- **Efficiency:** 98.3%
- **Features:** WiFi, FusionSolar App, 2 MPPT Trackers

### Battery Energy Storage
- **Model:** Huawei LUNA2000-5-S0

- **Total Capacity:** 10.24 kWh

- **Chemistry:** Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4)
- **Cycle Life:** 6,000 cycles @ 90% DoD

### Smart Metering
- **Model:** Landis+Gyr E460
- **Type:** 4-Quadrant Bi-Directional Smart Meter
- **Function:** Grid import/export monitoring, load profiling

- **WiFi Router:** TP-Link Industrial (Outdoor Rated, IP65)
- **CCTV Camera:** 2MP WiFi Camera with Night Vision
- **Function:** Visual monitoring of inverter and battery system
