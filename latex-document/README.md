## Document Structure
- **Title Page:** Project identification and classification
- **Executive Summary:** Key highlights and system overview
- **System Design:** Detailed specifications for PV, inverter, battery
- **Cost Breakdown:** Complete itemized costs by category
- **Performance Analysis:** Energy calculations and efficiency
- **Compliance:** South African standards (NRS 097-2-1, SANS 10142-1)
- **Installation:** Requirements, process, timeline
- **Operation & Maintenance:** Modes, monitoring, schedule
- **Warranty & Support:** Equipment warranties and technical support
- **Financial Analysis:** ROI, payback period, environmental impact
- **Appendices:** Diagrams, datasheets, checklists
